FAll Auditions


A new school year is kicking off! Looking to try something new?

Whether you’ve only two-stepped at parties or milly-rocked alone in your room, we’re here for you! Spend this quarter building your dance skills with crazy talented directors, making lifelong friends, and having the time of your life by joining one of our fall training teams -- no previous dance experience required! Some may say it’s an un-fall-gettable experience! (And we promise we are better at teaching you how to dance than we are at making puns...)

Foundations Choreography is catered towards beginning and intermediate dancers, so absolutely no experience is required! Expect training in dance fundamentals, choreography, and showcasing a set with a team that will grow with you as a family. Mandatory practices will be held twice a week for 3-4 hours in the evening, with no hell weeks or crazy obligations.

What better way to start off fall quarter than with a little throwback? The directors of Foundations 2017 fall training team Cerberus, Deandra Sumitro, an inspiring Foundations alum-turned-director, and Alex Tang of ACA, co-founder of Foundations Choreography, and director of fall 2016 training team Ra, have returned to teach our first Pre-Audition workshop! Curious? A tiny bit confused? We are too. But if one thing’s for real, it’s that this workshop is going to be a lot of fun. Come out to our first Pre-Audition workshop this Thursday, September 27th from 6-8PM in Lot 8 P2!
