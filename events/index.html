A new school year is kicking off! Looking to try something new?

Halloween, fallen leaves, pumpkin spice lattes. Fall is full of things that make people happy, so why not follow up on that trend with something that can top it all—dance! Not sure if you’re in love with dance just yet? Don’t sweat, our 2018 Fall Workshop Series is designed to help you figure that out! Whether you’re trying dance for the first time or returning after a long summer break, our Fall 2018 workshops are not only beginner-friendly, but are focused on fun, positive growth, and a perfect fit for every dancer!

By the end of the quarter, our 2018 Fall Workshop Series could have you trading in your cozy sweaters for dance clothes and your fall boots for sneakers. Keep the Halloween candy though, you’ll need the energy!

Stay tuned to this page and our social media as we reveal our lineup!

Have you ever wanted to learn new dance styles? Improve your freestyle? We have just the thing for you, Saturday Sessions! These sessions are intended to operate differently from a solely-choreo based workshop. Each session will operate differently depending on our session leaders and the topics they want to cover. You can expect exposure to different dance styles, freestyle concepts, drills, fundamentals, history, and some combos as well!

Schedule of Sessions:

10/27: Brandon Lee (ACA Hip Hop, FC Fall 2018: Plus Ultra, Director) & Patrick Tran (FC Fall 2018: Exodia, Director) – Freestyle concepts, floorwork
11/3: Alexxa Vasquez (Fam Biz, Samahang Modern) - House
11/10: Charlie Mann (NSU Modern, House of Balmain, FC Fall 2018: Peanut Butter, Director) & Ria Julian (ACA Hip Hop, VMo, FC Yurnt Director) - Waacking
11/17: Sam Park (ACA Hip Hop) - Popping

Who is invited:

These sessions are open to anyone in the UCLA community! These sessions serve as another platform for Foundations dancers to train, but are intended to enrich the UCLA dance community as a whole. We’ll love for you to join us!

Time and Place:

Saturdays from 3:30pm – 5:00 pm in John Wooden Center, Pyramid Room.
