Mission Statement

Our mission is to create an environment for beginning and intermediate dancers to grow, train, and challenge themselves.

History

Foundations Choreography was founded by Alexander Tang, Mrinal Chawla, Sophia Morgan, and Vincent Tang. Foundations began in Fall of 2014, when the founders recognized that the UCLA dance community lacked a space for new dancers to gain exposure to dance as well as train in a welcoming community. They started with free weekly dance workshops that academic quarter and formed training teams one quarter later in Winter 2015. Foundations is built on the notion that dance is for everyone, and aims to create an environment for beginning and intermediate dancers to grow, train, and challenge themselves.
